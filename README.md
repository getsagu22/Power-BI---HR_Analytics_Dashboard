HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

A comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI, leveraging advanced DAX calculations, Power Query data transformation, and interactive visual storytelling to help organizations track workforce trends and make data-driven decisions.

Project Overview - 
The HR Analytics Dashboard provides actionable insights into key HR KPIs such as:
Employee demographics
Attrition and retention trends
Department-wise headcount
Hiring & exit patterns
Attendance & leave patterns
Salary distribution and workforce cost
Tenure and experience analysis

This dashboard helps HR teams identify problem areas, optimize workforce planning, and support strategic decision-making.

Key Insights from the Dashboard -
Here are the major insights derived (customize based on your actual PBIX):
🔸 Overall attrition rate is highest among employees with tenure < 2 years.
🔸 Sales & Operations departments show the highest attrition.
🔸 Employees aged 25–35 form the largest workforce segment.
🔸 Job satisfaction scores are lower in mid-level positions.
🔸 Salary hikes are strongly correlated with employee retention.
🔸 Hiring increased by 18% YoY, but exits also increased proportionally.
🔸 Females show slightly lower attrition rates than males.
🔸 Employees who received higher performance ratings tend to stay longer.
